Privacy

Ypliet Denour as an artist values anonimity and wishes the same for their audience.


Email subscribers are safe from having their emails sold or leaked to the known extent of our security measures. There are no trackers or cookies on this website and there is no intent to include cookies. Any tracking embeded in the website is accidental and I encourage discussion in DMs at any time.

The option to save payment information or store personally identifying data will not be allowed on this site as it is an unreasonable risk for consumers. I, Ypliet, apologize for any inconvenience this may cause.

Please express any privacy concerns via discord or email and our team will do their best to tackle your problems head on.
