Are you learning to make music but lessons are too expensive? Does your workflow feel monotoneous or frustrating? Is being self taught holding you back from creating the sounds in your head?

These are things i struggled with for years. Enter the GUIDES page (can be accessed from the footer at any time) for answers to questions you didn't know you could ask. My goal with the guides page is to contest paywalls a novice writer will run into while navigating the music industry while also providing industry level talent knowledge that is gatekept by people who may fear that sharing knowledge will harm their future in entertainment.
